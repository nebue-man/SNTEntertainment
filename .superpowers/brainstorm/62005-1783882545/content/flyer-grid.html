How should event flyer cards be laid out?

Flyers will be multi-colored band posters with varying aspect ratios. The grid must feel cohesive despite the visual noise.

A

Fixed-ratio grid (Recommended)

All cards share a fixed aspect ratio container (e.g. 3:4 portrait). Flyer images are object-fit: cover inside — no matter how wild the original poster dimensions are, every card is the same size. Clean, disciplined, very neutral. Easiest to make cohesive.

B

CSS Masonry / Pinterest-style

Cards preserve their natural aspect ratio. Taller posters show more of the artwork. Creates an energetic, editorial feel. Harder to keep visually neutral — the varying heights create rhythm but can look chaotic with loud colors.
